How to Get Your Bridge Token

  1. Log in to your MeteorEdit instance
  2. Go to Profile > Preferences
  3. Under Cookie Bridge, click Generate Token
  4. Copy the token and paste it above
  5. Alternatively, click the one-click setup link in MeteorEdit to auto-configure this extension
